Welcome to SignGuru!

Introduction

At SignGuru, Discover a world where communication has no barriers, your gateway to Malaysian Sign Language interpretation and learning. Our innovative web application translates MSL into text and speech in real-time, enhancing daily interactions for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community in Malaysia. Our web application uses advanced machine learning to translate sign language into text and speech in real time, making everyday interactions more accessible for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. Powered by cutting-edge technologies such as MediaPipe, OpenCV, and TensorFlow, SignGuru offers a seamless and responsive sign language detection service directly from your web browser. Whether you're learning sign language or need an immediate way to communicate, SignGuru is here to assist you. Start using SignGuru today and experience the power of communication without limits.

Objective

  • Real-Time Detection:Translating Malaysian Sign Language into text and spoken language, making communication more accessible for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community in Malaysia.
  • Continuous Learning and Improvement: Provide resources for users to learn more about Malaysian Sign Language and offer features that help users improve their sign language skills over time.
  • Enhance Educational Resources: To increase educational resources for Malaysia's deaf-mute community sign language learning resources.

Features

Real-Time Sign Language Translation

SignGuru uses advanced real-time processing to translate Malaysian Sign Language into text and speech instantly.

Multi-Platform Accessibility

Designed with a responsive web interface, SignGuru is accessible on a variety of devices, including smartphones, tablets, and computers, without the need for additional software installation.

About

User-Friendly Interface with Educational Tools

The SignGuru application not only provides translation services but also includes educational tools and resources to help users learn and practice Malaysian Sign Language

Interactive Demo

Experience SignGuru in Real Time

Watch our interactive demo to see how SignGuru seamlessly translates Malaysian Sign Language into text and speech. This demonstration showcases the real-time capabilities of our application, running directly in a Python OpenCV window. Every movement and gesture is captured and interpreted on the fly, demonstrating the precision and responsiveness of our technology.


How It Works

  • Gesture Detection: Using MediaPipe alongside OpenCV, SignGuru captures each sign through a standard webcam setup.
  • Real-Time Processing: Our advanced algorithms, powered by TensorFlow and LSTM networks, analyze the gestures to detect and interpret the specific signs of Malaysian Sign Language.
  • Text and Speech Output: The interpreted signs are instantly converted into text and speech, allowing for immediate verbal communication and learning.

Why This Matters

SignGuru not only facilitates everyday communication for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community in Malaysia but also supports learning and education by providing a tool that can be used in educational settings, workplaces, and social environments.
