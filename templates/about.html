About

About SignGuru Website

SignGuru was developed as part of a final year project by an ambitious student in Electronic Engineering (Computer). Our mission is to leverage technology to facilitate communication inclusivity for all. This project combines the practical application of machine learning, web development, and embedded systems to create a robust tool that helps bridge the gap between the deaf and the hearing.

SignGuru is more than just a technological innovation; it's a commitment to inclusivity and community empowerment. By continuously refining our technology, we strive to support and enhance communication for the deaf and hard-of-hearing across Malaysia.

Our technology stack includes :

MediaPipe & OpenCV

These tools allow us to capture detailed hand gestures and movements with high precision, ensuring that every nuance of sign language is recognized and accurately translated.

TensorFlow and LSTM Networks

At the core of SignGuru is a powerful machine learning model that predicts and processes sign language sequences, turning dynamic gestures into comprehensible language.

Read More

Raspberry Pi 5

This cutting-edge device serves as the backbone of our web server, delivering low-latency performance and reliable uptime for users accessing our service globally.
